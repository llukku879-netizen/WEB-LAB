Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) is a private, co-educational minority institution established in 2002 in Mulavoor, Muvattupuzha, Kerala, by the Ilahia Trust. It's a pioneering self-financing engineering college, recognized by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU).

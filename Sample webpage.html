Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology

Lahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), Mulavoor P.O., Muvattupuzha is a co-educational minority institution approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU), and Mahatma Gandhi University, Kerala. The college is established in the year 2002.
